ABOUT SURGE

SURGE is a game-based platform that builds essential tsunami preparedness skills
through accessible 2D gameplay. Developed in collaboration with the Office of Civil Defense,
it turns disaster protocols into fun, interactive scenarios that teach through gameplay—
supporting UN Sustainable Development Goal 11 by promoting safer, more resilient communities.

LEARNING APPROACH

SURGE uses game-based learning to simplify complex tsunami preparedness protocols through interactive gameplay. Players build disaster preparedness skills and critical thinking by navigating missions, making choices, and learning through active play.

TARGET USERS

Designed for students, young adults, and residents in coastal areas, SURGE makes disaster preparedness accessible, engaging, and relevant—especially for communities at high risk.

CORE FEATURES

SURGE brings disaster prep to life with 2D gameplay, downloadable learning resources, task-based challenges, and accurate safety tips—all powered by official protocols from the Office of Civil Defense.
